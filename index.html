About Me

Hi, I'm Richard, a passionate software developer from Nairobi, Kenya. I am the founder of CodeKijiji, a software development company based in Africa. My mission is to bridge the gap between technology and local communities by delivering world-class solutions that have a lasting impact at the grassroots level.

With my passion in building custom software solutions, I am dedicated to helping businesses and communities harness the power of technology for growth and progress.
